Suggested Reading & Viewing for the Curious

Suggested Reading

None of this is required for the program — but if a topic grabs you and you want to go deeper, these are great places to start.

Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications

Richard Szeliski

A comprehensive, widely-used computer vision textbook. The author makes a draft freely available online.

Free online →

Deep and Shallow: Machine Learning in Music and Audio

Shlomo Dubnov & Ross Greer

An accessible introduction to machine learning and AI concepts through the lens of music and audio, with guided programming activities in each chapter.

Publisher page →

Computer Vision: A Modern Approach

David Forsyth & Jean Ponce

A classic, rigorous introduction to computer vision covering both classical and modern (learning-based) techniques.

Digital Image Processing

Rafael Gonzalez & Richard Woods

The standard reference for image processing fundamentals — filtering, transforms, segmentation, and more.

Neural Networks Series

3Blue1Brown

A beautifully animated, intuition-first video series on how neural networks, gradient descent, and backpropagation work.

Watch the series →
