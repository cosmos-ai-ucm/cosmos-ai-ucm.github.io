About This Cluster

The COSMOS AI at Scale cluster at UC Merced introduces students to the foundations of artificial intelligence through hands-on modules covering machine learning, computer vision, deep learning, and large-scale AI systems. This site collects the course modules, materials, and resources used throughout the summer program.
